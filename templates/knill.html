Knill's list of theorems in mathlib

Knill assembled a list of 172 elegant, beautiful, theorems in mathematics, which he describes as An expository hitchhikers guide to some theorems in mathematics. This page lists these theorems, and indicates which ones have been formalized in Lean.

To update this list, please submit a PR modifying docs/knill.yaml in the mathlib repository.
